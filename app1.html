Adobe Premiere Rush

Platform: iOS, Android, Windows

Multi-Track Editor: Yes

Export to Youtube: Yes

4k Support: Yes

An overview of the pros and cons of Adobe Premiere Rush.

Adobe Premiere Rush is a video editing app which is designed to be used by anyone who wants software that is easier and faster to use than the computer software from Adobe. This is an app for people that are new to editing and want to process their clips quickly and upload them to social media. The app is free and is available for iOS, Android, and Desktop. It has a nice and simple interface and large icons which is great for touch screen use. Typical tasks like adding videos and music is intuitive and easy to use. It offers 4 video tracks and 3 audio tracks to edit as well as the ability to add text, simple transitions, and filters to your videos. It also offers the ability to connect to your computer through Creative Cloud for more powerful editing.
